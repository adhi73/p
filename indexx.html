I don’t know how to begin this properly, so I’ll just be honest.

I messed up. I know that. I know my actions and my words hurt you, and I won’t insult you by trying to justify them. You didn’t deserve that pain.

What I want you to know is this — I see my mistakes clearly now. Not just that they were wrong, but why they were wrong. And that understanding has changed me.

I’m choosing to become a better person, not out of fear of losing you, but out of love for what we have and what we could still be.

Our love and our life together mean too much to me to repeat the same mistakes. I’m turning over a new leaf — patiently, genuinely, and for the long run.

I’m not asking you to forget anything. I’m only asking you to see the effort, the growth, and the sincerity behind it.

No matter where we stand right now, my feelings for you are real, steady, and unchanged.

Always,
